01Executive Summary

The Opportunity
No Solana perpetuals DEX currently offers team-based trading competitions. Adrena Squads fills this gap — introducing squad mechanics, skill-based scoring, spectator predictions, and compressed NFT achievement badges as an additive engagement layer over Adrena's existing infrastructure.

Adrena Squads addresses three specific weaknesses in the current competition system:

  • Solo-only format — limits organic growth, no recruitment loops
  • Whale-dominated leaderboards — absolute PnL rankings discourage retail traders
  • No spectator engagement — non-traders have no reason to watch or participate

The solution: squads of 2–5 traders compete on risk-adjusted (percentage return) scores. A spectator prediction market lets anyone stake USDC on competing squads. Achievement badges create long-term retention through progressive unlockables. The squad leader bonus creates viral KOL recruitment loops.

2.2 Competition Structure

Competitions are organized into weekly rounds within a broader season (8–10 weeks):

Season (8–10 weeks) ├── Week 1: Round 1 │ ├── Squad PnL calculated from on-chain trade history │ ├── Individual Mutagen accrues (existing system unchanged) │ └── Weekly rankings → Championship Points ├── Week 2–N: Repeating rounds │ └── Cumulative standings updated └── Final Round └── Season Champion = highest cumulative Championship Points

Championship points scale: 1st=100, 2nd=85, 3rd=70, 4th=60, 5th=50, then 40/35/30/25/20 for ranks 6–10, and 10 pts for 11th+.

03Scoring Mechanics

3.1 Formula

// Per-member score (stored as basis points on-chain) member_score = (realized_pnl / max_collateral_single_trade) × 10,000 // Squad aggregate (inactive members count as 0) squad_score = Σ(member_scores) / total_members // Example: 3 active members (+3200, +2500, +1800), 2 inactive (0, 0) squad_score = (3200 + 2500 + 1800 + 0 + 0) / 5 = +1500 bps = +15.00%

3.2 Why Percentage Return, Not Absolute PnL

ScenarioTrader A (Whale)Trader B (Retail)
Capital deployed$100,000$1,000
Realized PnL+$15,000+$200
Absolute PnL score +$15,000 (wins) +$200 (loses)
% Return score +15% +20% (wins)
Key Insight
Percentage return scoring makes competitions about skill, not capital. A $500 trader who returns 25% on their position outscores a $50,000 whale who returns 10%. This dramatically improves retail trader participation and reduces whale dominance.

3.3 Minimum Activity Requirement

Members who execute zero trades in a round score 0, dragging the squad average down. This creates an internal accountability mechanism — squads are incentivized to recruit and retain active traders, not passive stakeholders.

4.3 Squad Leader Bonus

Squad leaders receive 10% of their squad's prize allocation as a recruitment incentive. This activates KOL recruitment loops — content creators and community leaders are directly rewarded for building strong teams and bringing new traders to Adrena.

4.4 Season Championship

End-of-season prizes scale to 3× the weekly pool. Championship standings are computed from cumulative points across all rounds, rewarding consistent performance over lucky single-round outcomes.

4.5 Self-Sustainability

The prize pool is funded entirely from Adrena's existing 10% ADX buyback mechanism — no new token emissions, no unsustainable incentive programs. The competition creates volume → generates fees → funds prizes → attracts traders → generates more volume.

06Achievement Badges

Badges are stored as a u64 bitmask on the UserProfile account and minted as compressed NFTs via Metaplex Bubblegum — ultra-low cost (sub-cent per mint), transferable, and composable with the broader Solana NFT ecosystem.

BadgeUnlock ConditionBit
🏆 Squad ChampionFinish #1 in a competition0x01
🔥 Hot StreakWin 3 consecutive rounds0x02
💎 Diamond HandsLongest single-position hold duration0x04
⭐ Perfect RoundAll 5 squad members active in a round0x08
🤝 Squad BuilderCreate a squad that fills to 5 members0x10
🔮 OracleCorrectly predict the winning squad 3 rounds in a row0x20

The badge bitmask is owned by the program and set via crank — the user cannot self-award badges. The crank checks on-chain state (SquadEntry finalized + rank = 1 for Champion, etc.) before calling any badge mint CPI.

09Edge Cases & Abuse Prevention

Attack VectorMitigation
Wash Trading PnL-based scoring makes wash trading counterproductive — round-trips produce zero PnL. Volume is not a scoring factor.
Sybil Squads (1 person, N wallets) 50 USDC bond per squad (economic cost). Minimum 1 trade per member per round (operational cost per wallet). PnL scoring gains zero advantage from wallet splitting.
Colluding Squads Opposing positions means one side gets near-zero or negative score. Net EV of collusion is negative after 0.16% fees per close.
Inactive Members Zero-trade members score 0, dragging squad average. Leaders can kick after 2 inactive rounds. Slot remains empty — not refillable.
Squad Size Imbalance Score is averaged over total_members. A 2-person squad with elite traders beats a 5-person squad with inconsistent performers.
Late-Season Roster Gaming Squads lock at competition start. No mid-season changes possible on-chain.
Double Prize Claims ClaimRecord PDA (["claim", competition, claimant]) initialized on first claim. Anchor's init constraint makes a second claim physically impossible.
Prediction Self-Staking On-chain constraint: user_profile.current_squad != Some(squad_pubkey) enforced at the program level.

Prize distribution is trustless: once the Merkle root is posted on-chain, neither the program authority nor any other party can modify who receives what. Winners claim directly against the on-chain root using a SHA-256 Merkle proof. The ClaimRecord PDA prevents double-claims at the protocol level.
